Rabbit Blast Support

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Rabbit Blast is a single-player block puzzle game. For help, bug reports, or privacy requests, contact ygyim.biz@gmail.com. Support is available in Korean and English. We aim to respond within 3–5 business days.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. The current version does not require an account or login.

Where is my progress stored?

Progress, settings, and high scores are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app or its data may remove this information.

Does the game use purchases or subscriptions?

The current version does not provide in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Why do I see ads?

The app may show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads through Google Mobile Ads. See the Privacy Policy for more information.

What should I include in a bug report?

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, the affected screen, steps to reproduce the issue, and a screenshot if available. Do not send passwords, payment information, or other sensitive information.
