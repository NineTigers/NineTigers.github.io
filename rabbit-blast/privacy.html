Rabbit Blast Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

1. Operator and contact

Rabbit Blast (bundle identifier com.ninetigers.bunnyblast) is operated by Yin-goo Yim. For privacy questions, contact ygyim.biz@gmail.com.

2. Account and game data

Rabbit Blast does not require account registration or login. Game progress, settings, high scores, and similar gameplay state may be stored locally on your device to provide the game experience. The current version does not provide cloud save or a publisher-operated game account service.

3. Advertising and third-party services

The app includes Google Mobile Ads SDK for banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertising. When ads are served, Google and its partners may process device information, advertising identifiers, IP-address-derived approximate location, diagnostic and performance information, advertising data, and app or ad interaction data for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

Google's data practices are governed by its own policies. See the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses advertising technologies.

The app may present Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission where required. You can manage ad personalization and tracking choices through your device settings, subject to your device, region, and applicable platform controls.

4. Data sharing and retention

The operator does not operate a server-side player account or intentionally collect direct identifiers such as your name, email address, telephone number, or postal address through the app. Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete the app or its data. Advertising-related data is handled by Google and its partners under their policies.

5. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe that personal information has been provided through the app, contact us at the address above.

6. Changes

We may update this policy when the app's features, SDKs, or data practices change. We will update the effective date when material changes are made.
